How It Works

Copy the starter Google Sheet, add your household or group players, publish the Games, Plays, and Players tabs as CSV, then paste those links here. The app reads your sheets, but your data stays yours.

Beginner-friendly setup:
1. Copy the public Game Vault template sheet
2. Fill in the Players tab with `Player #` and each person’s name
3. Add games manually or import them into Games
4. Use the Game Vault → BGG Sync menu in Google Sheets
5. Publish the Games, Plays, and Players tabs as CSV
6. Paste those URLs into the fields below and click Save & Sync
7. Install the PWA on your phone if you want a homescreen app

Default public Plays columns:
Date · Game · Participants · Winner Player # · Notes · P1 Rating · P2 Rating · P3 Rating · P4 Rating · P5 Rating · P6 Rating
